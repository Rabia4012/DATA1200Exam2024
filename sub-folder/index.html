Mathematical Modelling and Data Science

Engineering - Bachelor's Program

Location Oslo
Duration 3 Years
Scope Full-time

The Mathematical Modelling and Data Science program at OsloMet combines mathematical theories with practical data science skills. This program is designed to equip students with the necessary tools to analyze and interpret complex data sets, and apply mathematical models to solve real-world problems.
